Compressed Formats

These are the FOURCCs I know about that refer to compressed formats (the ones that you see displayed when you don't have the right codec installed to play a given AVI file). If you know of any more, please drop me a line. You may also like to take a look at the following sites, all of which offer codecs for download or information on FOURCCs:

  • AFreeCodec.com - codec downloads, tools, discussion and other useful information
  • MovieCodec.com - codec downloads and other useful video-related information.
  • KM Studio - hosted in Ukraine. Contains an outstanding collection of downloadable codecs.
  • Quicktime Pixel Format FOURCCs - Apple's comprehensive list of codes used in Quicktime.
  • Codec-Download.com - free, downloadable codecs (also in German)
  • All Most Wanted Codecs (warning - adult content!)
  • Free-codecs.com - lots of codec downloads
  • Microsoft's FOURCC site
  • Wave and AVI Codec Registries - a Microsoft document from 1998 listing codecs

You may also like to look at my help page where this is a list of the codecs I have heard of but have absolutely no information on

Links from the "Name" column take you to sites devoted to the described codec and/or places from which the codec can be directly downloaded. Links from the "Owner" column take you to the corporate site of the owning company.
